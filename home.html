Welcome to Student profile

This portal helps students manage their profiles, view available courses, check grades, and share feedback — all in one place.


The purpose of this portal is to help students manage all their academic information in one place. It allows students to:
  • Create and update their personal profiles
  • Explore and learn from different courses
  • Check their grades and progress
  • Give feedback to improve learning
  • Easily communicate with teachers and classmates
